Early Age

Life in Ecuador

I (Jordan Ordonez Chaguay) was born in Guayaquil, Ecuador on April 10,2000. I am the son of Freinson Ordonez Castillo and Irlanda del Carmen Chaguay. I am the brother of Joselyn Ordonez Chaguay. I attended Escuela Basica Damasco until the age of seven.

Big Move

My family permanently moved to the United States on June 7, 2007. We resided in Stamford, Connecticut, living with my aunt's family for about a year. In the winter of 2008, we moved to the home where we would be for the next 14 years.
